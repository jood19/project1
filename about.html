About

The Gut Foundation is a charitable trust established to

Advance the education of New Zealanders about Gut diseases and improve, diagnosis
and treatment protocols for diseases of the Gut

Promote, conduct and publish research as part of the programme to improve, diagnosis and treatment protocols.

Provide financial assistance, equipment and other appropriate assistance for the purpose of facilitating the above objectives

We work with local medical people and local hospitals check our our Gut Health Hubs

The Trust is open to applications for specific projects.

We are a registered charity, so all donations are eligible for a tax rebate! Our registration number is CC28730.
